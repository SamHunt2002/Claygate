About Pelmear

Pelmear was founded in May 2022, with the intention of putting on new pieces of theatre aimed at challenging stigmas, breaking stereotypes, and providing a platform for creatives in theatre to explore and create. Our work is collaborational, and sees the ensemble as the ultimate tool for new ideas to grow and be explored.

Pelmear is committed to inclusivity and diversity within the arts. We want to work with everyone, without barriers or prejudice, on creating exciting pieces of theatre.
